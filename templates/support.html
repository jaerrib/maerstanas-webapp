Reporting Bugs

We strive to provide a seamless experience for our users, but occasionally, bugs may slip through the cracks. We appreciate your help in identifying and resolving these issues.

How to Submit a Bug

1. Using Codeberg (preferred):

  • Visit our Codeberg issue tracker.
  • Click on "New Issue."
  • Provide a detailed description of the bug, including steps to reproduce it, any error messages, and the environment you were using (e.g., browser, OS).
  • Attach any relevant screenshots or logs.

2. Via Email:

  • Send an email to jaerrib@tutanota.com.
  • Include a detailed description of the bug, steps to reproduce it, and any relevant screenshots or logs.
  • Mention the environment you were using (e.g., browser, OS).
